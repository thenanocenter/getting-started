What is Nano?

Nano is a digital currency for the real word - the fast and free way to pay for everything in life. It features instant transactions, zero fees and is infinitely scalable. Gone are the days of complicated and energy-inefficient transactions - Nano is green and easy to use.

What makes Nano?

Nano is a trustless, low-latency cryptocurrency that utilizes a novel block-lattice architecture, where each account has its own blockchain and achieves consensus via delegated Proof of Stake voting. The network requires minimal resources, no high-power mining hardware, and can process high transaction throughput.

Where to buy Nano?

Nano is listed on a few major cryptocurrency exchanges, such as Binance, Kucoin, and Nanex. On these exchanges you can buy Nano with BTC, ETH, USDT, DOGE and many more. If you prefer to buy directly with fiat, Nano is listed in LBX and N.Exchange.

Where to store Nano?

Nano has many options of desktop, mobile and web wallets. There is a wide array of choices when it comes to secure wallets. It’s up to you to pick your favourite.
